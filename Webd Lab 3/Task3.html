Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Bread slices

Instructions

Melt butter in a pan. Place bacon in the pan and cook until crispy. Remove bacon from the pan and set aside. Place cheese slices and tomato slices between two bread slices. Grill the sandwich until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted. Add bacon to the sandwich and enjoy!
